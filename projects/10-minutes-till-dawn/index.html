10 Minutes Till Dawn

How to Play

A very exciting fantasy survival game for you to play! Use WASD to move and dodge the creatures or else you'll loose hearts and die. Use your mouse to aim and click to shoot. You can collect the creature's coins when they die and upgrade your character to gain abilities!
